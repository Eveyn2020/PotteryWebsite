Hello Potters!

This potery class is perfect for begineers! You will learn everything you need to know about working width clay including how to prepare it, how the pottery wheel works and different throwing technique.

The 2-day(6 hours) workshop is a comprehensive session aimed at those who wish to have a more in-depth experiece, creating pieces which will be made uslingl all the fundamental ceramics discliplines if prinching, coling, slab, buildilng and throwing.

The workshop are structured to be fun and creative , and students can let their imagination run and wild during the session.
